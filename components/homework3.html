Flow Assignment using Three.js


About Visualization

We are working with a computational fluid flow simulation dataset from the San Diego Supercomputing Center. You can learn more about the science behind the simulation here: http://www.uni-kl.de/sciviscontest/

How to Interact?

  • To rotate the cylinder, hold the mouse trackpad and move it left, right, up, and down.
  • Scroll up and down over the Scene, to zoom in and out the data point cloud.
  • Drag the slider to move the plane along the Z-axis to see how the concentration of data points changes using XY Plane Slice Visualization.
  • By pressing the reset button, the configuration is returned to its initial state.
